About Us

At Grilli , we are passionate about delivering an exceptional dining experience to our guests. Located in the heart of Hengqin, we offer a unique blend of flavors and culinary traditions from around the world, showcasing the best of Western cuisine.


Our talented team of chefs brings together their expertise and creativity to craft a menu that caters to diverse tastes and preferences. Whether you're craving a juicy steak, fresh seafood, or a delightful vegetarian dish, our menu has something for everyone.


We take pride in using only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted local suppliers. Every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring a perfect balance of flavors and a visually stunning presentation. Our commitment to quality extends to our warm and attentive service, creating a welcoming atmosphere for you to relax and enjoy your meal.
